1- The iPhone X, released in 2017, features a 5.8-inch Super Retina HD display, a stainless steel frame, and a dual 12MP rear camera system. It was the first iPhone to feature a nearly bezel-less design with a "notch" for the front-facing camera and sensors, and it introduced Face ID for facial recognition. 
2-The iPhone 11, released in September 2019, features a 6.1-inch Liquid Retina HD display, an A13 Bionic chip, and a dual-camera system with 12MP wide and ultra-wide lenses. It also boasts an IP68 water and dust resistance rating, and is available in 64GB, 128GB, and 256GB storage options. 
3- The iPhone 12 Pro, released in 2020, features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, a Pro 12MP camera system, and the A14 Bionic chip. It also boasts 5G connectivity, a Ceramic Shield front cover, and an IP68 water and dust resistance rating. 
4-The iPhone 13 Pro is a high-end smartphone released by Apple in 2021. It features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology for adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz, and a triple 12MP camera system with advanced features like macro photography and ProRes video recording. Powered by the A15 Bionic chip, it offers powerful performance and is available in various storage options. 
5-The iPhone 14 Pro, released in 2022, features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology, offering adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz. It's powered by the A16 Bionic chip and has a triple-lens rear camera system with a 48MP main sensor, plus a 12MP front camera. The phone also includes an always-on display, Dynamic Island, and supports MagSafe and Qi wireless charging.
