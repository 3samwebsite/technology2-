What Is Computer

an electronic device that processes data, storing, retrieving, and manipulating information based on instructions. It accepts input, processes it according to programmed instructions, and produces output, while also storing the results for later use. Essentially, it's a versatile tool for performing calculations, managing information, and executing tasks through software.
